What Makes
Amrita School of Business Different?

Industry Certifications

We provide opportunities to get industry relevant certifications from leading companies such as IBM, Microsoft, SHRM, SAP, Six Sigma

High Tech-High Touch Interaction

An active mentor-mentee system with one-on-one interaction with the students ensures high touch environment as against the video recorded classes in an online program.

Peer-to-Peer Learning

Students are encouraged to connect with each other through various communities of students with specific interests.

Industry Connect

We have a colloquium series where industry people and our alumni contribute in teaching classes and projects. Amrita TBI plays an important role in this.

International Faculty

Faculty hail from Berkely, Cornell, XLRI, London School of Economics and Political Science, Florida State University, New York State University, University of Wisconsin-Madison, Nanyang Technological University, Telecom Ecole de Management, Deakin University and have rich industry experience with Fortune 500 companies such as Coca-Cola, IBM, etc.

International Certification
